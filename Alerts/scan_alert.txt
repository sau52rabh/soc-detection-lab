Alert Name: Port Scan Detection

Query:
index=* Sourcetype=WinEventLog://Microsoft-Windows-Sysmon/Operational EventCode=3
| stats count by SourceIp DestinationIp
| where count > 5

Trigger:
Real-time

Trigger Condition:
For each result

Trigger when:
Number of results > 0

Throttle:
Enabled

Throttle Field:
SourceIp

Throttle Time:
1 minute
